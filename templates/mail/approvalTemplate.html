What's next ?

Consortium member are required to create a ProximaX wallet to access BESC portal. Please follow below step to create a wallet


Step 1

Go to http://bctestnetwallet.xpxsirius.io . Press "Create Wallet" on top menu.

Step 2

Fill in "Wallet name", "Password", "Confirm Password". Then press "Create" button.

Step 3

If the wallet successfully create, it will display the word "Congratulations, your wallet has been created!". Now press on the "Show private key".

Step 4

Now you get your wallet private key and wallet address. Copy the text and store it in any place. It require wallet owner to remember the wallet private key and wallet address, please keep it safe.
